Our Story

Born from a Love of Dogs

Loving Homes was founded in Hong Kong by a lifelong dog enthusiast who believed that dogs should never have to compromise on quality of care when their owners travel. Frustrated by the lack of truly premium boarding options in the city, our founder set out to create a space that genuinely puts dogs first.

Since opening our doors, we have welcomed hundreds of dogs of every breed, size and personality — from energetic Labradors to gentle senior Pomeranians. Each one receives the same warmth, attention and individual care that we would want for our own dogs.

Our team is made up of qualified animal care professionals, trained veterinary assistants and lifelong dog lovers who genuinely cannot wait to come to work each day. We are proud to be Hong Kong's most trusted luxury dog hotel.
