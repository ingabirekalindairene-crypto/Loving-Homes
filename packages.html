Facilities & Packages

Everything your dog needs for a happy, healthy and deeply enjoyable stay with us.

Our Facilities

A World-Class Environment for Your Dog

Our purpose-built facility in Hong Kong has been designed with dogs' wellbeing at its heart — every space, every feature, every detail.

Grooming Parlour & Spa

Our professional grooming team provides full bath and brush treatments, coat trims, nail clipping and pampering spa treatments. Your dog will leave looking and feeling their very best.

Heated Rooms with TV

Every dog has their own private room with underfloor heating and cooling, a comfortable bed and a TV for company. Dogs sleep soundly in their own cosy space every night.

Chauffeur Collection & Return

Our premium chauffeur service will collect your dog from anywhere in Hong Kong and return them home at the end of their stay — completely stress-free and door to door.

Secure Outdoor Paddocks

Our fully fenced, secure paddocks provide safe outdoor space for dogs to run, explore and enjoy fresh air. Separate areas are available for dogs of different sizes and temperaments.

24-Hour Veterinary Services

A qualified veterinarian is on call around the clock. Any health concerns are addressed immediately, and owners are kept informed. Your dog's wellbeing is always our top priority.

Pet Watch

Our pet watch service provides continuous welfare monitoring throughout each dog's stay, ensuring any signs of stress, illness or discomfort are spotted and addressed promptly.

Woodland Walks

Daily guided woodland walks provide excellent mental and physical stimulation. Dogs love exploring the sights, smells and sounds of the natural environment on our beautiful walking routes.

Private Grass Areas

Our private grass areas give dogs exclusive outdoor time away from the group — perfect for dogs who prefer a quieter, more relaxed outdoor experience or for those who need extra space.

Indoor Play Barn

Our large indoor play barn is the perfect environment for active, social dogs. Packed with toys, climbing structures and interactive play equipment, it keeps dogs engaged whatever the weather.
